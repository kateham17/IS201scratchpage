BACKGROUND

  1. The Hunger Games began as a way for the Capitol to control the districts by:
    • Creating fear
    • Destroying hope
  2. Each year, the 12 districts must each supply 2 tributes
    • 1 male and 1 female
    • Between the ages of 12 and 18
  3. In District 12, Prim Everdeen and Peeta Mellark are selected
    • Prim's sister Katniss volunteers in her place
    • Sadly, Peeta's brothers don't volunteer for him
    • Now Katniss and Peeta must compete against the other 22 tributes for victory
  4. Before the games begin, Katniss and Peeta gain popularity
    • Cinna and Portia, their stylists, design unique outfits for them
    • Peeta announces that he has a crush on Katniss in his interview
